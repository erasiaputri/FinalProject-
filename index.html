What could CHIGORI be made of?

Chicken Cutlets

A chicken cutlet is simply a chicken breast that has been cut in half horizontally to make two thinner pieces. Often, the cutlet is also pounded out to make it even thinner so it cooks even quicker and more evenly.

White Rice

White rice varieties are usually tender and fluffy when cooked and they are used as a side dish or as an ingredient for a wide variety of main dishes.

Chilli

In Indonesian cuisines, chilli peppers give the dish richer taste, with a vibrant red color with the chili seeds visible and a slight piquant flavor to the spice.

Curry

A sauce seasoned with spices, which may commonly include turmeric, cumin, coriander, ginger, and chili pepper, among others.

Egg Whites

A part of the egg, but separated from the yolks which is the yellow core of the egg. Egg whites are used in the Salted Eggs Menu in which they are being salted beforehand

Spices

Adds aroma and taste to your dish. A whole lot of spices can be found and used in cooking a your dish.
